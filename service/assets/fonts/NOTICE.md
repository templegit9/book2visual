# Bundled font

**DejaVuSans.ttf** — used for burning captions into panels.

DejaVu Fonts are based on the Bitstream Vera Fonts and are released under a free,
permissive license that allows redistribution and use (including bundling in
applications). See the DejaVu Fonts license:
https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/License.html

Bitstream Vera and the public-domain Arev additions are likewise free to use and
redistribute. No copyleft obligations apply to the rest of this project.
